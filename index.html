Digital Strategy Design Lab (DSDL) is an innovative model for effective digital transformation.

DSDL is a 5-7 days workshop conducted by a government agency where the service beneficiaries, service providers and ICT experts work together to analyze manual services, design digital services and prepare implementation plan for the digital transformation of the agency.
